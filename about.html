ABOUT US

Our shop is located just south of downtown Los Angeles and features a showroom and fully-equipped woodshop. Paul Moorhead and a small team of woodworkers operate the shop Monday through Friday from 8am - 4:30pm. Our shop is currently open to the public for tours and information. If you'd like to speak with us about a project, please don't hesitate to contact us or visit our shop.

Woodworker
Christian studied furniture making at Cerritos College and also has an extensive background in painting, mixed media, and other arts. He has joined our team after working as a finish carpenter with the highly acclaimed Marmol-Radziner design firm. Christian is passionate about working with hand tools and the connection it provides with the material. He's also got jokes and livens up the atmosphere of the shop!
